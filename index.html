Our Mission

To provide our customers a unique, desirable products with the highest quality and the greatest standard of service through luxury, comfort, and constant improvement while staying true to our coastal culture.

Our Vision

To be a world leader in luxury fashion and lifestyle, as well as to lead in innovation while Contributing to the sustainable development of society and that of the environment with which we interact with.

How we began

Kisua Designs was an idea born from one of the founders, Dee and adopted by her partner Lydiah.The need to showcase our beautiful coastal culture through fashion is what catapulted the whole idea hence the slogan,Culture Pesonified.

What started out as an idea in the classroom has now come into being as one of the best and most sort after fashion house locally and globally as more people are looking at embracing their culture and wanting to be associated with Kisua .
